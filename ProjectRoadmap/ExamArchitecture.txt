1️⃣ Exam Engine – Core Tables

Từ schema project của bạn:

exam_templates
exam_template_sections
exam_template_items
exams
exam_questions
user_answers

Vai trò từng bảng:

Template Layer (Teacher thiết kế đề)
exam_templates
    └── exam_template_sections
            └── exam_template_items

Ví dụ:

Template: Grade 9 Midterm

Section 1: Grammar
    5 MC questions
    difficulty 2-3

Section 2: Vocabulary
    5 MC questions

Section 3: Reading
    1 passage + 5 questions

Template không chứa câu hỏi cụ thể
→ chỉ chứa rules để random.

Exam Instance Layer (Student làm bài)
exams
    └── exam_questions
            └── user_answers

Ví dụ:

Exam #301 (student A)

exam_questions
Q1 id=888
Q2 id=105
Q3 id=901
...

5️⃣ RPC #1 (quan trọng nhất)
generate_exam_from_template()

Đây là engine random câu hỏi.

Input:

template_id
student_id

Engine sẽ:

for each template_section
    for each template_item
        random questions
            category
            difficulty
            grade
            type

Insert:

exams
exam_questions
6️⃣ Example Flow

Template rule:

Grammar section
5 MC questions
grade = 9
difficulty 2-3
category = grammar

Engine query:

SELECT id
FROM questions
WHERE
    category_id = grammar
    AND difficulty BETWEEN 2 AND 3
    AND grade_level = 9
    AND is_active = true
ORDER BY random()
LIMIT 5

Insert:

exam_questions
